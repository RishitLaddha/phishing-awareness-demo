## 🔍 What Is This?

This is an interactive demo that simulates a **bank phishing attack**
so users can experience — in a safe, controlled way — how scammers
try to steal money and credentials.

It walks through:
1. A fake "urgent alert" mimicking real phishing tactics
2. An educational reveal explaining exactly what happened
3. Legal consequences under the **IT Act, 2000 (India)**
4. Practical tips to stay safe

---

## 🚩 Red Flags This Demo Teaches You to Spot

- Urgent / panic-inducing language
- Fake authority ("RBI is reviewing your account")
- Requests to transfer money to a "safe account"
- Asking for OTP or PIN
- Countdown timers to pressure quick decisions

---

## 🛡️ Golden Rules — Stay Safe Online

1. **Never share OTP/PIN** with anyone, ever
2. **Check the URL** before logging into any bank site
3. **Call the official number** on the back of your card if unsure
4. **Slow down** — urgency is a manipulation tactic
